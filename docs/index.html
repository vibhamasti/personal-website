Hi there 👋! I’m a senior undergraduate Computer Science and Engineering student at PES University, Bangalore. I learn to play the South-Indian instrument Saraswathi Veena in the Carnatic style.

My research interests lie in machine learning and related fields. I have worked on projects in the domains of data analytics, graph machine learning, semi-supervised learning, NLP and Music Information Retrieval. I also have experience interning at Vogo, Netradyne, and Goldman Sachs. My work has mainly been in the fields of software engineering, backend engineering and data engineering.
